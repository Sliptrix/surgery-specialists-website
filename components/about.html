About Dr. Francis A. Dysarz, III, M.D.

Dr. Francis A. Dysarz, III, M.D., is a highly skilled and experienced surgeon specializing in hand, wrist, and upper extremity surgery as well as reconstructive and microsurgery. With over 25 years of experience, Dr. Dysarz has established himself as a leader in his field, providing exceptional care to patients in St. Louis, Missouri.

Born and raised in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, Dr. Dysarz pursued his undergraduate and medical education at St. Louis University, where he earned a Bachelor of Arts in Chemistry and later his Doctor of Medicine. His advanced training includes fellowships in Hand and Microvascular Surgery at both the University of South Florida in Tampa and the Hand and Microsurgery Medical Group in San Francisco, California.

Dr. Dysarz is a diplomate of both the National Board of Medical Examiners and the American Board of Surgery, with certifications in General Surgery and Surgery of the Hand. He is an active member of the American College of Surgeons and the American Society for Surgery of the Hand, ensuring he stays current with the latest advancements in his field.

Beyond his exceptional medical skills, Dr. Dysarz is fluent in English and medically conversational in Spanish, providing a comfortable experience for patients with diverse linguistic backgrounds. He is a dedicated husband and father to five children.
