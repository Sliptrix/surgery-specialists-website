About Dr. Dysarz

With over 13 years of specialized experience in St. Louis, Dr. Dysarz has successfully treated thousands of patients with upper extremity conditions. His expertise encompasses both surgical and non-surgical approaches to hand, wrist, and elbow disorders.

Dr. Dysarz is committed to providing personalized care, utilizing the latest techniques and technologies to ensure optimal outcomes for his patients. He takes the time to thoroughly explain conditions and treatment options, ensuring patients are well-informed partners in their care.

Our practice is dedicated to restoring function and reducing pain, helping patients return to their daily activities and pursue their passions without limitation.
